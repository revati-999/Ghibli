CHARACTER DESIGN

Shizuku Tsukishima

Shizuku is a slender fourteen years old girl, with short umber brown hair. She is inquisitive and a voracious reader, who longs to be a writer when she grows up. One day she notices that all of her library books have previously been taken out by one Seiji Amasawa.

Seiji Amasawa

Seiji Amasawa is an ambitious and driven young man who aspires to become a master violin maker just like his grandfather. Seiji's character embodies the spirit of dedication and the pursuit of excellence, influencing not only his own life path but also catalyzing significant growth in those around him.

Shizuku's Family

Shizuku has four members in her family .Shiho Tsukishima is the first born daughter of Seiya Tsukishima and Asako Tsukishima and the older sister of Shizuku. Shizuku's family is mostly supportive but also concerned about her decision to follow her passion for writing which is met with a mix of support and worry

Seiji's Family

Seiji's most influential family member is his grandfather, Shiro Nishi. Shiro is a loving and wise old man who owns an antique shop where many of the film's pivotal moments take place. He is also a skilled violin maker, and his work significantly influences Seiji's aspirations.

Building the World

Whisper of the Heart beautifully showcases the emotions of young teenagers who are at a pivotal point in their lives. The movie revolves around Shizuku, who is on a quest to discover her secret admirer, and Seiji, who aspires to become a master violin maker . As their paths cross, they fall in love and inspire each other to pursue their ambitions. This heartfelt narrative explores themes of love, personal growth, and the pursuit of one’s dreams.
